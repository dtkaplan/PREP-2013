Travel routes and the trade-off between time and distance

These are the data collected by the participants in 3 minutes from Google Maps, looking at various routes from Hesston College KS to Goshen College IN. They entered distance (miles) and time (minutes longer than 14 hours) via a Google form. The data were then downloaded from Google and are plotted here.

routes = read.csv("Indiana-Kansas.csv")
plotPoints(Distance ~ Travel.Time, data = routes, pch = 20, xlim = c(0, 
    150), ylim = c(780, 880))

Each dot is one possible route between Hesston and Goshen.
